We are delighted to share the publication of “Musicking, Peacebuilding, and Consciousness,” by Research Fellow Michael Golden and myself. It took us more than a year to get it done, but we enjoyed the process and it was all worth it! This article is part of a special issue of the Journal of Consciousness Studies on the topic of “Multidisciplinary Developments in Mind, Music, and Mental Health.” It was guest edited by our colleagues Simon Høffding and Suraiya Luecke.
